Choose what type of learning course you want to create

Microdegree

A Microdegree is a mini training course that someone can undertake online. They can be either free or paid trainings and work very effectively as lead magnets for your online funnels.

Certification

Create a Certification to award a certificate of completion to someone who completes the course. This is ideal if you're training trainers, facilitators, or awarding students with a certificate for completing your course. Certifications should enable the student to 'do' something with what you're teaching. Students will also receive a badge on their profile.

Microcourse

This is like a Microdegree, except you can schedule the release date of individual steps. Do this if you'd like to release your course over time, or have students take it in cohorts.

Microschool

Microschools allow you to create a whole course and embed existing Microdegrees into the course for students. You can release steps over time, and involve faculty in teaching your Microschool. If you're running a Microschool, you should be teaching the students that enroll - this is more hands-on learning than a Microdegree.
